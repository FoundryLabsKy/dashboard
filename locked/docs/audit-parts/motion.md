**Summary (census):** 93 inline `transition:` declarations (~45 on touchable elements) plus 5 global rules (L1163/1165/1168/1172/1210) that put fixed-duration curves on 100% of buttons — 6 distinct cubic-beziers, zero JS springs, zero velocity inheritance.
Keyframes: 44 total — 8 entrance/sheet, 9 feedback/attention, 27 tutorial/lock-finale; the 6 `slideUp .5s` sheet entrances are the worst §11 #2 offenders.
JS-timer pseudo-animations: ~30 (≈15 toast/save-confirm auto-dismiss, 5 typewriter/step-sequencing, 3 clocks, 2×16ms edge auto-scroll loops, 5 keyboard-scroll hacks) — none respect reduce-motion (L1162 covers CSS only) and none are interruptible.
