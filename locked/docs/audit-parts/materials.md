**Summary:** Census: 16 true glass (backdrop-filter) surfaces = 10 nav/control bars (1 bottom Nav + 9 headers/input bars, all legitimate layer roles, 3 blurring nothing) + 5 blurred modal scrims + 1 tinted toast; plus 12 plain dim scrims, 1 near-opaque rest banner, 1 gradient edge scrim, and 3 translucent info-card fills (no glass on content cards found — brand rule holds). The two Criticals are the total absence of `prefers-reduced-transparency` fallbacks (§11 #12) and glass-stacked-on-glass from the blurred toast/scrims compositing over glass bars (§11 #6), followed by text-on-tinted-glass in the check-in toast (§11 #5) and a brittle, partially dead attribute-selector delivery mechanism. Everything except the scroll-edge shim and the sheets-get-glass brand decision is a Safe, localized CSS fix.
